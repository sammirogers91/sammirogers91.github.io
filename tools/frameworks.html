My response:


I picked Kube.


Kube is said to be slightly heavier than Pure but lighter than Bootstrap by miles. It's website says (and very well demonstrates) that the framework emphasizes minimalism and focuses on typography, with its unique approach to horizontal rhythm by utilizing a 4px vertical grid. I personally would use this framework because I really appreciate the minimal design aspect, and I fell in love with its styling of forms, especially its use of color-coded two different states of 'success' and 'failure' in response to inputted values.
